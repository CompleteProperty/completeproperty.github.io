A Commitment to Excellence at Every Level

Our construction projects have shaped – and continue to shape – the skylines of cities such as Atlanta, Boston, Miami, Tampa, San Diego, Minneapolis and New Orleans. It is our long established track record for excellence combined with over 25 years of experience that have enabled us to build such an impressive portfolio. How have we achieved this stellar track record?

Strong management skills and a dedicated work ethic that are shared by every member on our team. At CPS, we train and develop some of the best experts in the industry that are fully committed to the job. We make sure that these values also extend to our partners and to our long-term trusted contractors.

Late delivery of work is simply unacceptable. In fact, in 25+ years of our history, we can proudly say that we have never missed a completion date due to issues that are within our control! Our principal executive personally oversees and manages weekly project reviews for every single one of our clients where time, budget, safety and quality are regularly and consistently monitored.

We hold ourselves accountable! Our clients know that they can count on us even under the tightest time frames. We never take shortcuts and refuse to place profits over our client’s best interests and over the safety of our team.

Safety. When it comes to safety, there is zero tolerance and no compromise. Read more about our commitment to safety.

Construction projects do not always unfold in a linear fashion and at Complete Property Services we have the experience to meet any challenges that may arise. Proper planning and open communications with our clients and team members enable us to handle highly demanding and technical projects with complete success.

A commitment to excellence at every level is why our clients look to us for their construction and renovation needs and that is why over 90% are repeat customers that come to us again and again for their projects.
