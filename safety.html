Committed To Safety

Our safety record is something that we are very proud of and is one of the best in our Industry. This translates to lower than industry average insurance experience mod ratios which translates to “peace of mind” and more competitive prices for our clients. Safety plays a fundamental role in our company and it’s our absolute commitment. Our safety policy is much more than just a per-project implementation or a per-site consideration: it is a way of life that has become part of our corporate fabric and is at the forefront of all our initiatives.

Our zero tolerance philosophy starts right at the very top with our leadership and works all the way through to our field personnel and subcontractors. Our Loss Control policies have been derived from the wisdom and experience of our professional staff along with our associates in the industry.

Our personnel are trained to be proactive when it comes to safety and have learned to deal with potential problems before they arise. All of our projects are inspected daily making sure that we are always in compliance with the current OSHA and ANSI standards. Moreover, we employ a full time safety director who, unannounced, visits sites and reports findings directly to our President. When a sub-contractors is engaged, they must sign our Corporate Safety Policy, as part of their contract and are held to the same standards as our own personnel.

In addition to training, team members are screened to ensure a substance-free workplace.

It is our ethical and moral responsibility to ensure the safety of our team, partners and clients. Making sure that at the end of the day we have protected our clients and everyone goes home safe.
